Overview

Primandir ("we", "us") is building a daily spiritual practice app. This page explains, at a high level, how we approach privacy for this website and for the app as it is developed. We aim to collect as little personal information as we can.

This website

This website is a simple, static informational site. When you choose to join our waitlist or book a conversation, you submit your information through forms and scheduling tools provided by HubSpot. The information you enter (for example, your name and email address) is processed and stored by HubSpot on our behalf so we can contact you about early access. Please refer to HubSpot's own privacy practices for details on how they handle that data.

We do not currently run advertising or cross-site tracking on this website. If we add website analytics in the future, we will update this page first.

The app

Primandir is being built to be local-first. Many things you do in the app — such as your practice history, reflections, and personal notes — are intended to be stored on your device. The app may offer an optional account so you can back up or sync your data. Where the app uses services such as Firebase for optional accounts, sync, crash reporting, or privacy-conscious analytics, those services process data on our behalf.

We design the app so that private free-text content you write — such as reflections, mood notes, prayer text, and feedback messages — is not used as the content of analytics events.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use your data for third-party advertising networks.
  • We do not knowingly collect information from children in violation of applicable law.

Your choices and account deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data from within the app. On-device data is removed when you delete it on your device. If you have an account, synced copies associated with your account are scheduled for removal; this may take some additional time to fully clear after deletion is confirmed.

Contact

For privacy questions, please use our contact page.
