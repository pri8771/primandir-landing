What is Digital Temple?

Digital Temple is a daily spiritual practice app being built to help you show up for ritual, prayer, reflection, and learning — gently and consistently. The goal is a calm, modern home for your practice, not another feed to scroll.

We're early. Features described here are planned or in active development, and details may change as we listen to early-access members.

What you'll be able to do

A few of the experiences we're building for early access.

  • 🪔

    Guided ritual

    Step-by-step daily practices and rituals you can follow at your own pace.

  • 🙏

    Prayer

    A growing library of bundled prayers with editorial introductions and context.

  • 📓

    Reflection

    Private, on-device space to note how a practice felt and track your own consistency.

  • 📚

    Learning

    Short, approachable lessons to deepen your understanding over time.

  • 🎧

    Audio & meditation

    Guided audio for meditation, sleep, and focus to support your practice.

  • 🤝

    Community

    Planned ways to practice alongside others and mark festivals together.

Who it's for

  • People who want a steadier, more intentional daily spiritual practice.
  • Anyone reconnecting with ritual, prayer, or reflection on their own terms.
  • Busy people who want something calm and simple, not overwhelming.
  • Early adopters who'd like to shape an app while it's being built.
